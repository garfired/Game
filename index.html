Monster Survivors is a thrilling online survival game where you battle against endless waves of monsters. Upgrade your skills, collect powerful weapons, and strategize to stay alive as long as possible.

Controls & Shortcuts

Master these controls to dominate the battlefield:

  • WASD - Move character
  • Mouse - Aim & Attack
  • Spacebar - Dash / Evade
  • Q / E - Use special abilities
  • P - Pause the game
